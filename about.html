SSM Salt Mine

SSM, located in Mohammedia, Morocco, is one of the highest quality deep salt mines in the world. SSM is currently the only shaft mine of it’s kind shipping into North American Market. Many of our customer say SSM rock salt is the highest quality salt they have ever used. We can produce SSM rock salt to any specification our customers demand. Moisture content is consistently less than 0.5% placing it among the driest mined rock salt in the world. Sodium Chloride content ranges from 97.5% – 99.5%. SSM bulk road salt also meets ASTM D-632 Type 1 Grade 1. SSM Rock Salt has a pinkish/tan color which enables our customers to control the application rate by being able to distinguish where they have spread their salt.
